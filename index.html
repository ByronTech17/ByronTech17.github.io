Byron Springer

Byron K. Springer is the President of the European School of Karlsruhe Pupils' Committee and CoSup (Conseil Supérieur des Elèves) Representative & Treasurer.

Byron lives in the Black Forest and is in S6 in the English section. With the slogan 'It's time for a Change' he ran and won the Pupils' Committee presidency for the Schoolastic year 2022-2023.

With 3 past generations completing their european baccalaureate at the European School of Karlsruhe Byron has seen the school evolve and grow into what it has become.

In times of challenge and change, Byron’s help in leadership ushered in a stronger school community, a better school environment, and school more respected around the European School system. The Years so far were ones in which more students not only began to see themselves in the changing face of the European School of Karlsruhe, but to see the European School of Karlsruhe the way he always has – as the only school where so many of our stories could even be possible.

It's time for a Change.
