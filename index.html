About Byron Springer

Byron K. Springer is the Vice-President of CoSup Gaming and a PC member since 2020.

Few students have walked a more improbable path to the European School of Karlsruhe. Born in Germany to a mother from South Africa and a father from Germany, Byron was raised together with three other siblings whose generosity of spirit reflected their South African and Asian roots. The homespun values they instilled in him, paired with his innate sense of optimism, compelled Byron to devote his life to giving every student, regardless of his or her background, the same chance the European School of Karlsruhe gave him.

After working his way through school for 10 years with the help of his families support, Byron lives in the Black Forest, where he has won many awards and also has helped many different aspects of the European School system for students being developed. That experience honed his belief in the power of uniting ordinary people around a politics of purpose, in the hard work of studentship, to bring about positive change. At CoSup Gaming, he became the youngest vice-president.

Since 2017, Byron has been actively involved in many different school projects and has been working on many different projects to boost students potential and productivity around the school.

In times of challenge and change, Byron’s help in leadership ushered in a stronger school community, a better school environment, and school more respected around the European School system. The Byron years so far were ones in which more students not only began to see themselves in the changing face of the European School of Karlsruhe, but to see the European School of Karlsruhe the way he always has – as the only school where so many of our stories could even be possible.
