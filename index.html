Byron K. Springer is based in Cardiff, Wales, United Kingdom with experience in live events and theatre as FOH engineer, Monitor engineer build-up crew (stage and cable management) and lighting designer for various event scenarios such as corporate meetings, live music, theatre and some AV background for broadcasting and videoconferencing.

As a first year student at the University of South Wales, Byron is currently completing his BSc (Hons) in the course Sound, Lighting and Live Event Technology with the aim of becoming an expert in creating seamless live event experiences accesible for everyone. Byron currently aims to specialise in sound after his bachelors degree.

Byron always looks forward to keep learning, meeting new peers and enjoying the "audio, video and lighting for event adventure"!

Byron has had the pleasure to work with companies and event locations like the Conseil Supérieur des Élèves (CoSup), the European School of Karlsruhe, Germany, Hermann-Hesse-Gymnasium Calw, Germany, Jans Showtec, Beck Event Gruppe and many more. Night Clubs, Theaters and Restaurants like Finn's Penthouse, Mainz, Germany, Den Atelier, Luxembourg and many more.
