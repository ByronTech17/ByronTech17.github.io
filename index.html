Byron Springer

Byron K. Springer is the Vice President of CoSup Gaming and a PC member since 2020.

Byron lives in the Black Forest and is in S5 in the English section. Throughout his academic career, Byron has won multiple awards. As a young leader, Byron has helped to instill purpose in many, not only by way of his ability to unite others, but also his collaborative energy. Championing what is right for others comes easily to Byron and is largely a result of his innate optimism and positive outlook on life.

That experience honed his belief in the power of uniting ordinary people around a politics of purpose, in the hard work of studentship, to bring about positive change. At CoSup Gaming, he became the youngest vice-president.

In times of challenge and change, Byron’s help in leadership ushered in a stronger school community, a better school environment, and school more respected around the European School system. The Byron years so far were ones in which more students not only began to see themselves in the changing face of the European School of Karlsruhe, but to see the European School of Karlsruhe the way he always has – as the only school where so many of our stories could even be possible.
